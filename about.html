What is the pomodoro technique?
  • A time management method.
  • Involves breaking work into intervals called "pomodoros"
    (usually 25 minutes).

    • Each pomodoro is followed by a short break,
    (5 minutes).
  • After completing four pomodoros, take a longer break
    (15-30 minutes).
  • Helps maintain focus and productivity by providing structured work and rest periods.
